Student Programs

Meet our Student Programs, and how they can help you throughout your journey at BMCC!

     

ASAP

Committed to earning your Associates Degree within three years? Consider ASAP. As an ASAP student, you will benefit from comprehensive and personalized advisement, academic support services, professional development opportunities, tuition assistance, transportation and textbooks. You will be part of our ASAP family and well on your way to personal and academic success. 
​
 Program Benefits
  • A free unlimited MetroCard
  • Assistance with textbooks
  • An ASAP Scholarship for eligible students
  • A dedicated ASAP advisor
  • Take classes with other ASAP students
  • Career and support services ​

College Discovery

 College Discovery (CD) is a program that supports BMCC students so that they can achieve their goals in college and beyond. College Discovery works with BMCC students so they have the skills to do well in their classes, the financial help they may need, and personal and/or academic counseling. 



Program Benefits
  • In-person tutoring in a variety of academic subjects
  • E-tutoring from home
  • Appointments with a CD counselor
  • Personal Enrichment Program (PEP) Workshops
  • CD Toolkit to assist you in your courses
  • Use of College Student Indicator (CSI) to identify moods.

Conexiones & Connections

The Conexiones & Connections program is open to all students who are interested in exploring and refining their purpose through their college experience. In addition to serving all students, the program offers a community of support for males of color. Our Spanish-speaking staff members offer bilingual support for our Latinx
students. 

Program Benefits
  • A personalized plan and support team developed to meet your goals
  • Bilingual support from staff and peer mentors
  • Events* to meet and interact with other students
  • Assistance with career-oriented employment and internships
  • Financial literacy workshops and counseling in Spanish
  • Civic engagement and service learning providing Co-Curricular Transcript (CCT) credit
  • Conexiones & Connections Leadership Academy
  • Transfer preparation
  • Free winter or summer course (subject to program conditions, requirements, and funding availability)​ ​

Year Up

Year Up is an intensive, one-year program for students working towards their associate degree,  combining hands-on skill development and internships at some of America’s top companies. As you work toward completing your degree at BMCC, we support you in launching a professional career in business, finance, or technology.


​
Program Benefits
  • Support in launching a professional career
  • 35 hours/week in a professional internship during second semester
  • internship partners include JPMorgan Chase, Bank of America, American Express, and many more
  • 85% of Year Up alumni are employed and/or attending college full-time within four months of graduation and earn an average annual income of $44,000.

Degree Under Three


​
The Degree Under Three program was created to assist students who are committed to graduating in under three years. In this program, you will be part of a group (cohort) of students who are working towards the same goals as you: to graduate faster. You will have a team of personal coaches and academic advisers to help you plan your courses and ensure you have the support needed to succeed inside, and outside, the classroom.

Program Benefits
  • Being part of a group (cohort) of students who have the same goal of graduating in under three years
  • Your own academic success coach who will have one-on-one meetings with you to provide guidance and to address any concerns you may have
  • Workshops to help you achieve your academic and professional goals
  • Career counseling, to help you identify the best possible career path
  • Monthly email check-ins with your coach to help you stay on track
  • Tuition-free summer/winter courses (subject to program conditions and requirements)
  • Financial Incentives (subject to program conditions and requirements)
