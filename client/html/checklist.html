Obtaining a New or Replacement ID Card

ID card printing is currently available to all registered students and active employees at the Service Desk located in room S-141 of 199 Chambers street, weekdays from 8:00am to 8:00pm.

Every BMCC student and staff member must have a validated identification (ID) card at all times. Members of the BMCC community must have their ID card to enter the college, to enter the library, to borrow library books, or to collect student paychecks from the college. Students are expected to present their ID card to any college official (security officer, administrator, faculty and staff member, etc.) who requests such identification.

your ID card is not working, please visit the Service Desk in person at room S141.

Obtaining a New ID Card

New students can obtain a BMCC ID card from the Service Desk located at room S-141. Students must be registered for classes before they can obtain an ID card and must provide a form of photo identification. Newly hired faculty and staff can obtain a BMCC ID card from the Service Desk after Human Resources processes their information in CUNYFirst and they are entered into the ID system. Faculty and staff must provide a form of photo identification as well as an authorization slip from Human Resources, located at room S-717. Research Foundation and contractor employees must be entered into the ID system by the Office of Sponsored Programs before they can obtain an ID card, and must provide both a form of photo identification and an ID request form from their supervisor to obtain an ID card from the Service Desk.

Replacing a Lost ID Card

A signed ID replacement form is required to obtain a replacement ID card. The form must first be brought to the Bursar's Office, room S330, and a $10 replacement fee must be paid. Then bring the signed ID replacement form to the Service Desk, room S141, to obtain a replacement ID card. The ID replacement form can be found here

Borrowing Equipment

The Library has laptops, tablets, graphing and scientific calculators, and other equipment. Equipment can be borrowed from the Circulation Desk.

To borrow equipment from the Circulation Desk, students must have a valid BMCC ID card. The CUNY appropriate computer usage policy applies to all computer usage, including use of Laptops and Tablets.

Distance Learning Devices

Chromebooks, iPads, and Bluetooth keyboards are available for loan for currently registered students who need these devices for distance learning. They can be borrowed until the end of the semester. Current information about device pickup and return is available on our blog.

Laptops

  • They can be checked out a half hour after the library opens.
  • Last checkout is a half hour before closing.
  • Laptops can be borrowed for three hours at a time and can be renewed once.
  • Anything you wish to save must be saved to a USB drive. To print from laptops, please see our instructions.

Other Devices

  • Tablets
    Tablets can be checked out a half-hour after the library opens. They can be borrowed for three days and can be renewed once.
  • Flash Drives
    A small number of USB flash drives are available to use with Library equipment. They can be borrowed from the Circulation Desk.
  • Calculators
    We have several dozen scientific calculators. They can be checked out for eight weeks. Graphing calculators are available to be checked out for seven days.
  • Battery Chargers
    Chargers are available for three-hour loan. While the chargers work with most devices Apple users are advised to bring their own cable to plug into the charger.

Policies

Equipment is subject to overdue fines of $15 for every hour late up to a maximum of $150. Replacement and repair fees may also be charged if the equipment is damaged. For more information please see our Policies Page.
