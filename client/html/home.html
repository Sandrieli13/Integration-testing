About Our Website
Welcome to our website! We understand that navigating the original BMCC website to join clubs and connect with mentors may be a troublesome task as information is not easily available. That's why we have embarked on a mission to make this process more accessible and user-friendly. Our goal is to enhance the functionality of the clubs website by providing a platform that allows students to effortlessly join existing clubs or create new ones. Our simplified login system allows students to easily explore and discover a wide range of clubs and mentors that align with their interests. We are confident that our website will not only streamline the process but also foster meaningful connections between students and mentors.
New student Community

Description :
    Our solution for the new student community is a comprehensive website that provides all the necessary information and resources related to their degree work in their majors.
Here are the key features and functionalities of our solution:

Curriculum search functionality:
    One of the key features of our website is a robust curriculum search function. This allows students to quickly search for specific courses or requirements within their major. They can easily find information about course availability, prerequisites, course descriptions, and schedules. This feature aims to save students time and reduce the chances of missing important registration deadlines by providing a user-friendly and efficient way to navigate the curriculum.

Quick assistance:
    We prioritize providing quick and efficient assistance to students through our website. This includes features such as a live chat support system, where students can connect with advisors or knowledgeable staff members to get immediate help with their queries or concerns. We also offer a comprehensive FAQ section that addresses common questions and issues students may encounter, allowing them to find answers without the need for direct assistance.

Data Analysis Visualization:
    To aid students in selecting their majors within the CIS department at BMCC, our solution includes a dedicated page featuring a set of data analysis visualizations. These visualizations provide an overview of the majors offered, including information on enrollment trends, employment prospects, average salaries, and other relevant data points. By presenting this information in an easily understandable format, students can get a quick glance and gain insights into different majors, helping them make more informed decisions about their academic path.
Career Readiness
Description :
    The problem of career readiness stems from a lack of practical skills, limited professional networks, inadequate career guidance, and limited exposure to the job market. Our solution aims to address these problems by providing students with the necessary resources, support, and opportunities to enhance their career readiness, increase their employability, and successfully transition into the workforce.

*Chatbot assistance: We have integrated a chatbot into the webpage, which is available 24/7 to answer students' questions and provide assistance.

*Data Analysis Visualization: To aid students in selecting their majors within the CIS department at BMCC, our solution includes a dedicated page featuring a set of data analysis visualizations.

*Assistance in skill development: The website will provide students with resources and materials to enhance their skills relevant to their desired career paths.

*Job search support: The website will provide students with job search support by offering a job board with curated job listings, resume and cover letter templates, and resources on job market research.
